About Shreya Bakes

Freshly baked cakes, cookies and brownies, crafted daily in Dubai with care and precision

Shreya Bakes started with a simple idea, make pastries that actually feel homemade, not mass-produced. Everything we do is baked fresh in Dubai and delivered with care.

We focus on taste first, design second. Because a pastry should look good, but it should taste even better.

What we’re about

Most of our orders come from real moments, birthdays, weddings, last-minute surprises. That’s what keeps things interesting. No two days are the same.

We don’t run a factory. We run a kitchen. Everything is made fresh, nothing is frozen, and every order gets attention.

What matters to us

Fresh ingredients. Consistency. And making sure what you receive feels worth it.

We also keep things flexible, custom designs, dietary needs, and special requests are part of what we do every day.
